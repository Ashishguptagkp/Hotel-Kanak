Welcome to Kanak Hotel, where luxury meets convenience in the heart of the city. Dive deep into Ayodhya, a place of pilgrimage, nerve centre of India’s heartland and storied birthplace of Shri Ram. With an array of comfortable and affordable stay options, including the above homestays and rooms to book and more, Ayodhya is the place for a reset, family holidays, history tours and more. Local attractions include Hanuman Garhi Mandir and Fort, Ramkot, the Hanuman Garhi Fort, the Nageshwarnath Temple, Brahmakund, Ram Ki Paidi, the Kanak Bhavan Temple, Ayodhya Ghaat, the Vijayraghav Mandir and the banks of the Saryu River.
